Peri Peri Chicken

This peri peri chicken recipe smothers chicken in a homemade peri peri sauce then bakes or grills them and serves them with extra sauce. It's a quick and easy weeknight meal.

    FOR THE PERI PERI CHICKEN:
  • 6 Chicken Legs
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
    • FOR THE PERI EPRI MARINADE:
  • 1 pound red chillies chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic chopped
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 cup cilantro chopped
  • 1/4 cup basil chopped
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Juice from 1 lemon
  1. Marinade the Chicken
  2. Grill in the Oven for 75 min at 450 F
  3. Serve with shredded parsely on top
  4. Gobble it down and enjoy
